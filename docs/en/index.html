    Getting Started

    Comprehensive & Modern DICOM Solution

    From image storage to professional viewing, Brigidprovides a complete toolchain to handle your medicalimaging data.

    Professional DICOM Viewer

    Integrated with BlueLight Viewer to provide a smooth web-based medical image viewing experience.

    DICOM & DIMSE Support

    Supports DICOMweb (WADO-RS, QIDO-RS, STOW-RS)and traditional DIMSE services (C-STORE, C-FIND).

    Sharing & Workspaces

    Generate permission-controlled sharing links withpassword protection and multi-workspace isolation.

    Modern Tech Stack

    Brigid is built with a modern technology stack, making it a reference for learning web-based medical imaging architectures.

    • Tanstack Query & Zustand

    Backend
